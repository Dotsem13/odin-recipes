Spaghetti

Spaghetti with ground beef and vegetables

Home made spaghetti with lots of ground beef and healthy vegetables,

a nice comforting meal that is easy to make, and everyone loves.

Ingredients

  • 1 pounds ground beef
  • 500 grams sspaghetti pasta
  • 500 grams mushroom, sliced
  • 3 bell pepper, diced
  • 4 carrots, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 medium union, chopped
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon chicken herb
  • 1 can tomato sauce
  • 1 can tomato paste

Steps

Step 1

First bake the chopped union and the minced garlic, then add the mushroom, the bell peppers and the carrots.

Step 2

Now add the ground beef and cook it till its wel done.

Step 3

Add the tomato sauce and the tomato paste.

Step 4

Start cooking the water for the pasta, now you can add all the seasoning Ingredients.

Step 5

When the water cooks, add the pasta for 9 minutes, so it cooks al dente.

Final Step

Now server the freshly cooked pasta, with the sauce and finish with some parmesan cheese.

Enjoy your meal.
